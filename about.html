Samay Raina

Stand-Up Comedian & YouTuber

Hi, I'm Samay Raina. I'm an Indian stand-up comedian and YouTuber, born on October 26, 1997. I'm the co-winner of Comicstaan 2 (2019) and currently host the comedy talent show India's Got Latent.

I grew up in a conservative Kashmiri Pandit family in Jammu. I moved to Pune for a print engineering course, but honestly, it felt like a waste of time. Instead, I started doing open mics in August 2017 and quickly became a regular in the local comedy scene. After performing in Pune, I moved to Mumbai to pursue comedy full-time.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, I started streaming chess on YouTube, which unexpectedly blew up. I've played with chess legends like Viswanathan Anand, Garry Kasparov, and Magnus Carlsen. My chess rating on Chess.com is around 1621, but I've peaked at 1942. I've won tournaments like the Botez Bullet Invitational and SuperPogChamps, and I donate my winnings to causes I care about. I also started online chess tournaments called Comedians On Board, featuring my comedian friends and other celebrities.

I've collaborated with many musicians, appearing in music videos for Seedhe Maut's "11K" and KRSNA's "Sensitive."

In 2025, I went on a nationwide tour, "Still Alive And Unfiltered," and in 2026, I became one of the youngest Indian comedians to perform at Madison Square Garden. I released my debut comedy special, Still Alive, on YouTube in March 2026, which became the most viewed stand-up comedy special on the platform.

I'm currently working on two collaborations with Netflix India—a second season of India's Got Latent and a new stand-up special.

Of course, my journey hasn't been without controversy. I've faced backlash for jokes and the India's Got Latent show, but I continue to push forward with my comedy and content creation.

Thanks for stopping by!
