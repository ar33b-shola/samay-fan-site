Samay Raina

Audience Rating: 10/10 | 1997 | Never Boring

Samay Raina is a prominent Indian stand-up comedian, YouTuber, and internet personality who rose to massive fame for popularizing online chess streaming in India during the pandemic and later creating the viral talent hunt show India’s Got Latent. Born in Jammu into a Kashmiri Pandit family, his sharp wit, edgy humor, and unique ability to build online subcultures have made him one of the country's most successful independent content creators.

Profession: Comedian Origin: Jammu
